AngularFaces 2.0 tutorial step 06 - PrimeFaces datatable or AngularJS Datatables?

Datatables

PrimeFaces datatable are incredibly powerful, but they don't integrate with AngularFaces. They work in an AngularFaces view, but they don't benefit from it.

This chapter shows you what you can do with the PrimeFaces datatable, and explores some of the client-side alternatives.
